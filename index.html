HOW TO PLAY
1. Garage — Unlock cars with Race Points (RP), then repaint them with the color pickers.
2. Circuit — Pick one of three tracks. Difficulty affects width and opponent skill.
3. Race — W/↑ gas, S/↓ brake, A/D or ←/→ steer. 3 laps against 3 AI drivers.
4. Score — Finishing position + your time convert into RP, saved to your driver name.
5. Stats — Speed cars hit higher top end; Control cars corner tighter without losing grip.
